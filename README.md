# paint-industry-powerbi-analysis
Power BI dashboard analyzing financial performance of leading Indian paint companies (Asian Paints, Berger Paints, Kansai Nerolac) from FY2021–FY2024.
# 🎨 Indian Paint Industry Analysis – Power BI Project

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes the financial performance of major Indian paint companies using Power BI.

Companies analyzed:
- Asian Paints
- Berger Paints
- Kansai Nerolac

The dashboard evaluates revenue growth, profit growth, and profit margins between **FY2021–FY2024**.

---

# 📈 Industry Overview

## Market Trends
- Indian paint market growing at **~9–10% CAGR**
- **Decorative paints dominate with ~77% share**
- Rising **urban housing and infrastructure demand**
- Increasing demand for **premium and eco-friendly paints**
- Growth in **industrial and automobile coatings**

---

# ⚡ Tailwinds & Challenges

## Tailwinds
- Housing demand
- Infrastructure growth
- Eco-friendly product adoption

## Headwinds
- FY25 revenue slowdown
- Raw material price volatility
- Increasing competition

## Investments in Industry
- Birla Opus entering market with **₹10,000 Cr investment**
- Expansion investments from **JSW Paints**
- Strategic changes from **AkzoNobel**

# 📊 Dashboard Insights

## Asian Paints
- Net Profit Margin: **12.1%**
- Revenue Growth: **7.0%**
- Profit Growth: **21.6%**

## Berger Paints
- Net Profit Margin: **40.4%**
- Revenue Growth: **63.0%**
- Profit Growth: **58.5%**

## Kansai Nerolac
- Net Profit Margin: **9.3%**
- Revenue Growth: **14.7%**
- Profit Growth: **49.6%**
